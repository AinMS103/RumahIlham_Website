HISTORY

Pertubuhan Rahoma Darul Fakir Malaysia is a non-profit organization established on November 21, 2008 and is registered with the Department of Social Welfare (JKM). Rahoma Darul Fakir provides shelter, education as well as religious, spiritual and physical wellness to orphaned, underprivileged children from low-income families. Rahoma now has 2 care homes, located at Jalan Kent, Kuala Lumpur and Subang Jaya, Selangor. Rahoma also assists single mothers in entrepreneurship for generating their domestic income. To add, this organization have been independently managing these orphanage homes that runs on public funds and donations.
